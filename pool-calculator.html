SWG chemistry notes

FC minimum with CYA

Keep FC ≥ CYA÷7.5 to avoid algae. At CYA 70 ppm, minimum FC = 9.3 ppm. Shock (SLAM) level = CYA × 0.4 = 28 ppm.

Salt cell efficiency

Cell works best at 3000–3500 ppm. Below 2700 ppm the cell may shut off or alarm.

pH drift in SWG pools

Salt cells produce slightly alkaline byproduct. pH tends to rise — check twice weekly and add muriatic acid as needed.

Shock (SLAM) threshold

Shock level = CYA × 0.4. At CYA 70, shock FC = 28 ppm. Hold until CC < 0.5 ppm and pool is clear.

Muriatic acid safety

Always add acid to water, never water to acid. Pour slowly near a return jet with the pump running. Wait 30 min to retest.

Salt cell maintenance

Inspect cell every 3 months. Clean calcium buildup with dilute muriatic acid (1:10 ratio) when output drops.
